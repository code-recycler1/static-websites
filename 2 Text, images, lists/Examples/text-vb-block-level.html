A smooth start at Thomas More!

At Thomas More, you start your first academic year with 'welcome days'. On these days, we serve a perfect combination of cozy relaxation activities and interesting info sessions. These info sessions are a must, they are part of your training program. This way, you get to know your fellow students, your campus, and your course even before you have had a single lesson.
